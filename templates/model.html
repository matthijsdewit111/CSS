Computational model



Random walk condition

  • N random walkers released from a random point at y = 0
  • When a walker hits a neighbour of the cluster, growth occurs
  • The walkers are destroyed and redeployed when they reach y = ymax
  • Periodic boundary conditions






Diffusion condition

  • Steady state solution
  • Growth weighted by the converged concentrations
  • N iterations of growth
  • Periodic boundary conditions

Pruning branches

  • Conditional removal of leafs
  • Lifetime between 5 and PS system iterations old
  • Each qualified leaf is removed with probability 0.4
  • The Pruning Span (PS) has a relative large influence on the shape
